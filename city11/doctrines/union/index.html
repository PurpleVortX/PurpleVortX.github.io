UNION DOCTRINE

SECTION-01

INTRODUCTION

UNION is one of the many field divisions a UCA Protection Unit may go into after joining the UCA.

SECTION-02

MANNERS & BEHAVIOUR

The UNION unit is the face of the Universal Union. While tactical units (RAZOR/EOW) are the fist, UNION is the constant reminder of Order. Any display of “Human” deviation (humor, anger, hesitation) is a failure of conditioning and is grounds for points deduction or socio-rehabilitation. Remember, you are the placeholder for the Will of the Benefactors. If you act like a human, the Citizens will treat you like one. If you act as an instrument of the Union, they will obey.

SECTION-03

RANKS & EQUIPMENT

The UNION supports upward momentum. Advancement through the ranks of Civil Protection is expected and facilitated for compliant personnel demonstrating operational effectiveness.

Progression is conditional, not guaranteed. Any misconduct, procedural deviation, or failure of discipline will be recorded and acted upon accordingly. Advancement may be delayed, revoked, or terminated at the discretion of oversight.

High Command Echelon

Welcome to High Command. You have advanced to the highest operational tier within Civil Protection. This position represents the maximum extent of authority granted to human command personnel.

Remember: You do not rule the City. You administer it on behalf of the Universal Civil Authority. Every order issued at this level carries weight beyond the precinct and will be measured against Combine doctrine. There is no further promotion; only continued service or removal. Your role is not privilege, but responsibility under constant oversight.

Important Note: Elite Protection Units from the Academy are considered High Command for UNION, and therefore do hold power over lower units.

The Adjutant Commander will be appointed by the Field Commander and is to act in their absence.

SECTION-04A

SQUADRONS

Below is a list of every Squadron you can be a part of.

➤ PILLAR - Stand and Hold
PILLAR units are static enforcement specialists tasked with holding critical infrastructure and population choke points. Trained to withstand prolonged resistance, they prioritize area denial, barricade control, and unwavering presence. When a district must not fall, PILLAR stands until relieved or terminated.

➤ HOUND - Find. Secure. Remove.
HOUND squadrons are specialized search-and-seizure enforcement units tasked with locating unauthorized individuals, contraband, and resistance assets. Utilizing coordinated sweeps, informant data, and relentless pursuit protocols, HOUND units enter, secure, and clear target zones with minimal delay. Once identified, assets are confiscated and subjects are removed for reassignment or processing. Evasion is considered noncompliance.

➤ SENTINEL - The Eye That Never Blinks

SENTINEL units serve as persistent surveillance, monitoring districts for behavioral anomalies and unauthorized activity. Through constant patrols, sensor integration, and silent observation, SENTINEL ensures that nothing goes unseen—and nothing unreported.

SECTION-04B

DEPLOYMENT PROTOCOL

All units within a squadron may be deployed at a Squadron Leader’s discretion, however, the situation must permit.

PILLAR

➤ PILLAR units may be deployed in response to civil unrest or confirmed rebel activity. Units are not to be recalled until the situation is deemed fully pacified, after which all issued heavy armaments must be surrendered.

➤ APC deployment may only be authorized by the assigned Squadron Leader. Due to their offensive and defensive capabilities, APCs must be deployed with discretion and require two units for operation. Authorization conditions include extended rebellion, fortified resistance, or the confirmed presence of heavy weaponry.

HOUND

➤ HOUND units may be deployed as required for barricaded suspects, contraband seizures, or confirmed rebel hideouts. Units are granted 404 access, ensuring no physical barrier is considered impassable.

➤ Breaching authorization must be issued by a high-ranking officer. Upon approval, the leading unit is permitted to force entry and conduct a full clearance of any designated area.

SENTINEL

➤ SENTINEL units serve as standard patrol and monitoring forces, maintaining continuous presence and reporting irregular activity within assigned districts.

SECTION-05

VIOLATIONS & POLICIES

All units are expected to operate strictly within assigned authorization levels and active counter-procedures. Unauthorized use of force, equipment, or clearance access constitutes dereliction of duty and will be logged for review.

Failure to comply with established protocols, command hierarchy, or operational limitations may result in disciplinary reassignment, memory replacement, or prosecution. Order is maintained not through freedom of action, but through absolute adherence.

LEVEL 01 - MALCOMPLIANT ACTIVITIES

[1.1] - UNPROFESSIONALISM

➤ Behaving, chatting, or speaking in a manner deemed unprofessional

[1.2] - INTENTIONAL DISOBEDIENCE

➤ Intentionally disobeying, ignoring, or going against orders from those possessing Field Commander, rank members of any Field Division’s Command or High Command, or Officers within the Oversight

[1.3] - UNNECESSARY CONVERSATION

➤ Engaging in conversations unrelated to assigned duties with citizens or other Protection Units when such conversations are a distraction or interrupt the assigned duties

[1.4] - LOITERING

➤ Idling near protocol-restricted zones or remaining idle after given directives

VERDICTS:

  1. Verbal Warning
  1. Administrative Warning
  1. 4-day prohibition
  1. Division blacklist

LEVEL 02 - MALCOMPLIANT ACTIVITIES

[2.1] - ENCOURAGING REGULATION BREACHES

➤ Encouraging citizens or other Protection Units to break protocol or commit a violation of any level

[2.2] - FALSE ACCUSATION

➤ Accusing citizens or other Protection Units of violations that were not committed

➤ Upon report of this violation, a command echelon JURY unit will be assigned the case to perform a hearing involving all users. Said hearing must be within all involved Units’ available time

[2.3] - VOICELINE SPAM

➤ Spamming voicelines intentionally is due for verdict

VERDICTS:

  1. Administrative Warning
  1. Game Kick
  1. 30-day prohibition
  1. Division blacklist

LEVEL 03 - MALCOMPLIANT ACTIVITIES

[3.1] - DISOBEDIENCE TOWARDS COMMAND

➤ Citizens who blatantly ignore directions or orders are due verdict.

[3.2] - EVADING PROSECUTION

➤ Resisting a verdict or detainment is due Capital Prosecution.

VERDICTS:

  1. Administrative Warning
  1. 30-day prohibition
  1. Division blacklist

SECTION-06

SOCIOSTATUS

There are several sociostatuses that affect our glorious City. UNION members are REQUIRED to follow direct orders before procedure.

➤ CITY LOCKDOWN

This counter-procedure is activated by a Squadron Leader or higher when more than one precinct is occupied by a hostile force.

When a City Lockdown is in effect, all border activity will be terminated. Units are to enforce Violations as normal, and any threat identified should be pacified.

➤ JUDGEMENT WAIVER

This counter-procedure is activated by a Division Leader or higher when more than one precinct is occupied by a hostile force.

When a Judgment Waiver is in effect, all border activity will be terminated. Units are to order citizens inside, and any violation during this time results in Capital Prosecution.

➤ AUTONOMOUS JUDGEMENT WAIVER

This counter-procedure is activated by an Adjutant Commander or higher when more than one precinct is occupied by a hostile force, and is not deactivated until the City is deemed pacified.

When an Autonomous Judgement Waiver is in effect, all border activity will be terminated. Any citizen or rebel identified during this time will be subject to pacification.

➤ ADVISORAL ASSISTANCE ACT

This counter-procedure is activated by an Oversight Officer or higher when deemed appropriate.

When the Advisoral Assistance Act is in effect, all units will be subsumed into the Overwatch Transhuman Arm. Under this sociostatus, units are to pacify the City at all costs. Units are granted 404 Access, and tools provided to the OTA division to assist with this task.

SECTION-09

DISCHARGING

HONORABLE DISCHARGE

Any ranking member of UNION may be honorably discharged at their own discretion, given they meet the following criteria:

➤ Passed recruitment exam

➤ Obtained less than three breaches of protocol

DISHONORABLE DISCHARGE

Any ranking member of UNION may be dishonorably discharged at their Superior’s discretion, given they meet one or more of the following criteria:

➤ Have already obtained limited prohibition

➤ Amputated a superior unit
